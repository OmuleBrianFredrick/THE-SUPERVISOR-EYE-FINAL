# PROJECT EXPANSION OBJECTIVE

Supervisor Eye must not only function as a hierarchical reporting and supervision system for client organizations, but must also operate as a **multi-tenant SaaS platform** where we, as the core developers and platform owners, maintain centralized oversight and business control over every organization that registers.

The system must therefore include **two CRM layers**:

---

# LAYER 1 — MASTER PLATFORM CRM (Developer / System Owner CRM)

Design and implement a **centralized Master CRM Dashboard** controlled exclusively by us (the platform developers / Super Admin owners of Supervisor Eye).

## Purpose:

This CRM will allow us to monitor, manage, and control every company, organization, or business that creates an Executive Account on Supervisor Eye.

## Core Functions:

### 1. Organization Registration Monitoring

When any Executive user registers and creates a new organization account:

* Automatically capture:

  * Organization Name
  * Executive Name
  * Industry Type
  * Country / Region
  * Contact Email
  * Phone Number
  * Registration Date
  * Subscription Plan
  * Account Status (Active / Trial / Suspended / Pending)
* Display this information in the Master CRM dashboard.

---

### 2. Client Lifecycle Management

The Master CRM should support:

* Lead tracking
* Trial organizations
* Active paying clients
* Subscription renewals
* Payment history
* CRM notes
* Assigned internal account manager
* Upgrade / downgrade plans
* Suspension or deactivation controls

---

### 3. Revenue & Financial Monitoring

The Master CRM must include:

* Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
* Active subscriptions
* Payment alerts
* Outstanding balances
* Invoice generation
* Organization-by-organization revenue analysis

---

### 4. Internal Administrative Controls

Super Admin (us) should be able to:

* Approve or reject new organizations
* Assign onboarding packages
* Configure CRM templates
* Monitor usage statistics
* See number of users per organization
* View organization activity logs
* Push system-wide announcements

---

# LAYER 2 — AUTO-GENERATED ORGANIZATION CRM (Client CRM)

When a new Executive successfully creates an organization:

### CRM Modules:

### Organizational Hierarchy:

Executive → Managers → Supervisors → Workers
